    • Our Story

    The founder's family has had a history of dementia, where generations of people have been full-time caregivers at home.

    Dementia usually bring enormous time and financial commitment for the whole family, who are especially vulnerable during the COVID-19 pandemic and under a severe shortage of professional care facility. As the number of Dementia and Alzheimer's patients grow rapidly worldwide, there needs to be an affordable and effective way to help.

    This is what the "A beaufitul Day" app is trying to achieve. We are harnessing the power of technology to take care of our elderlies and families. We want to make the interface as simple as possible, connect and exchange information with caregivers, and create an approachable companion for the patients when they are alone.

    A group of undergraduate software developers, business leaders, and neuroscientists at Johns Hopkins University are brainstorming and iterating our designs. We also work closely with the Johns Hopkins Alzheimer's Disease Research Center to create the best mobile smart assistant possible.
